\begin{abstract}
	\noindent
	Eunoia is a logical framework designed for
	specifying the proofs and proof systems of SMT solvers,
	namely \cvc{5}.
	%
	We present a translation from a core fragment of Eunoia
	to the \emph{$λΠ$-calculus modulo rewriting} as implemented
	by the LambdaPi proof assistant.
	%
	The translation is implemented by our tool \texttt{eo2lp},
	which we use for generating LambdaPi encodings of
	%
	(a)~a large fragment of the
	\textit{Cooperating Proof Calculus} (CPC),
	the Eunoia signature defining \cvc{5}'s proof system, and
	%
	(b)~proofs produced by \cvc{5} on \texttt{unsat}
	problems from various fragments of SMT-LIB.
\end{abstract}

\section{Conclusion and Future Work}\label{sec:conclu}

We have presented a translation from Eunoia to the
$\lambda\Pi$-calculus modulo rewriting that encodes
\cvc{5}'s proof system automatically, unlike
approaches that hand-write soundness lemmas for each
rule~\cite{coltellacciReconstructionSMTProofs2024a,mohamed25leansmt}.
The trade-off is that the translated rules are taken
as axioms rather than proved sound; their
well-typedness and subject reduction are
nevertheless verified by LambdaPi. Our tool
\texttt{eo2lp} translates all 22 modules of CPC-MINI
and independently verifies 97\% of 1{,}036 proof
scripts across 14 SMT-LIB fragments.

Relative to Ethos, the reference checker, our
pipeline is slower (0.47\,s vs.\ ${<}0.1$\,s
average) and supports a subset of CPC, but adds two
semantic guarantees Ethos does not give:
well-typedness of inference-rule signatures in a
type system with formal metatheory, and subject
reduction of side-condition programs---the latter
surfaced upstream CPC bugs that Ethos missed because
it only typechecks program clauses at application
time (\autoref{sec:results}).
The most direct future work is extending CPC-MINI
to the full CPC (bitvectors, strings, datatypes,
reals) and a like-for-like comparison with Ethos.
Because the encoding lives in LambdaPi, it further
provides a basis for proving individual CPC rules
sound directly in $λΠ$, and---in the longer
term---a route via Dedukti to Rocq, Isabelle/HOL or
Lean, analogously to
IsaRare~\cite{lachnitt24isarare}; those downstream
translations vary in maturity, so this is an
architectural bet rather than a turnkey pipeline
today.
More speculatively, the typed interpretation of
\autoref{sec:lambdapi} could serve as a working
formal semantics for Eunoia until an official one
appears, and a setting in which to study the
higher-order extensions proposed for
SMT-LIB~3~\cite{barrettSMTLIB3Bringing,smt3-proposal}.
